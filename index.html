Hello and Welcome

I have been absorbed with voice acting since I first booted up Starcraft in 1999 and listened to some of the most immersing voice acting I had heard at the time.
It has stuck with me through the years and inspired me to sit in a dark room and scream into a microphone.
My credits include many various video game characters and trailers, as well as all sorts of commercials and radio work.

Skills & Services

  • Character Voices for Video Games and Animation
  • Commercial Voiceovers for Radio, TV, and Online Ads
  • Narration for Audiobooks, Documentaries, and E-learning
  • Voice Acting for Dubbing and Localization Projects
  • Script Interpretation and Direction
  • Directed live sessions via Discord, Source Connect, Zoom, FaceTime
  • My set up includes a broadcast qualioty studio, Audient iD4 Interface, and AT4040 Mic
