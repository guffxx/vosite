Hello and Welcome

My name is David and I have been absorbed with the power of voice since I first booted up Starcraft in 1998 and listened to some of the most immersing voice acting I had heard at the time.

Alongside Film and TV, Video Games have stuck with me through the years and inspired me to sit in a dark room and scream into a microphone.

Voice acting is not only a a profession but a passion. The pride of a moving performance is my drive to create memorable moments for the audience.

My credits include various video game characters, monsters, narrators and more. I have also been the voice of radio commercials, virtual slot machines, and audio books.

If you would like to get in touch, please send me an email at: guffre@icloud.com, or DM me on social media.
